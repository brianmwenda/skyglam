What Is Event Decoration?

Fabric decorators explore the world around them through a distinctive visual lens.  Fabric decorators coordinate fabrics with pattern, color, mood, and a sense of rhythm to achieve a harmonious balance. You will learn how to use these skills to create mood/style boards for your portfolio, highlighting current trends in fashion and the aesthetic qualities of textiles; texture, color, and pattern. Learn how to apply your talents as stylists do by  applying your unique flair to creations in apparel, fashion, and lifestyle. This course will help you develop an Eye for Styling
